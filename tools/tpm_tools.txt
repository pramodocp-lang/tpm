TPM INTERVIEW ANSWER

Q: What tools have you used as a TPM?

A:
I have worked with Jira for Agile project management, sprint planning, backlog management, and tracking project delivery. I use Confluence for documentation, requirement management, and knowledge sharing. For reporting and data analysis, I use Microsoft Excel and Power BI to create dashboards, track KPIs, monitor project health, and provide stakeholder updates. For communication and collaboration, I regularly use Microsoft Teams and Slack to coordinate with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.

A TPM uses these tools to improve planning, execution, tracking, reporting, communication, risk management, and stakeholder visibility throughout the project lifecycle.
