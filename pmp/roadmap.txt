PHASE 5: PMP FORMULAS
=================================================

Earned Value Management (EVM)

CV = EV - AC
SV = EV - PV
CPI = EV / AC
SPI = EV / PV
EAC = BAC / CPI
ETC = EAC - AC

Communication Channels

n(n-1)/2

PERT Formula

(O + 4M + P) / 6

Where:
O = Optimistic
M = Most Likely
P = Pessimistic
