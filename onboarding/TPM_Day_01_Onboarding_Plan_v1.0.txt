# Technical Project Manager (TPM) - Day 1 Onboarding Plan

Project:
Enterprise Legacy System Migration & Modernization

Role:
Technical Project Manager

Objective:
Become familiar with the organization, understand the project at a high level, build initial relationships, and gather information without making process changes.

=================================================================
09:00 AM - 09:30 AM
HR & IT Onboarding
==================

Objectives

* Complete onboarding formalities
* Receive company assets
* Verify system access

Checklist

□ Laptop configured
□ Email account activated
□ VPN access
□ Jira access
□ Confluence access
□ GitHub access
□ Slack / Microsoft Teams access
□ Calendar access

Expected Output

* All required systems are accessible.

=================================================================
09:30 AM - 10:00 AM
Meeting with Reporting Manager
==============================

Objectives

* Understand expectations
* Learn current priorities
* Clarify responsibilities

Questions

* What are the top business priorities?
* Which projects will I own?
* What are the team's current challenges?
* What should I achieve in the first 30, 60, and 90 days?
* How do you define success for this role?

Expected Output

* Role expectations documented.
* List of assigned projects.
* Initial priorities captured.
