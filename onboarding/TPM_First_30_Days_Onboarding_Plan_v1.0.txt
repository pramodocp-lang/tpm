# Technical Project Manager (TPM) - First 30 Days Onboarding Plan

Project:
Enterprise Legacy System Migration & Modernization

Document Version: 1.0

---

## OBJECTIVE

The goal of the first 30 days is to understand the business, build relationships, learn the product, 
understand the technical landscape, and establish trust with the team.

The focus is:

* Learn first
* Observe carefully
* Build relationships
* Understand the delivery process
* Avoid making major process changes too early

---

## WEEK 1 - LEARN & OBSERVE

Objectives

* Complete company onboarding
* Obtain access to all required tools
* Understand the organization structure
* Meet key stakeholders
* Learn the product at a high level

Tasks

□ Laptop and system setup
□ Email access
□ Jira access
□ Confluence access
□ GitHub access
□ Slack / Microsoft Teams access
□ Development environment overview

Read

□ Project Overview
□ Project Charter
□ Product Roadmap
□ Business Requirements
□ Architecture Overview
□ Sprint Board
□ RAID Log

Meet

□ Reporting Manager
□ Product Owner
□ Engineering Manager
□ Scrum Master
□ Solution Architect
□ QA Lead
□ DevOps Lead

Observe

□ Daily Stand-up
□ Sprint Planning
□ Sprint Review
□ Sprint Retrospective

Deliverables

* Team Introduction Notes
* Stakeholder List
* Initial Observations

---

## WEEK 2 - UNDERSTAND THE PROJECT

Objectives

* Understand project goals
* Learn delivery process
* Review project documentation
* Identify dependencies

Review

□ Project Scope
□ Timeline
□ Milestones
□ Risks
□ Current Sprint
□ Release Plan

Understand

* Team responsibilities
* Development workflow
* Testing process
* Deployment process
* Approval workflow

Questions

* What are the current priorities?
* What are the biggest challenges?
* What is blocking delivery?
* What are the project risks?
* What are customer expectations?

Deliverables

* Project Understanding Document
* Stakeholder Mapping
* Initial Risk List

---

## WEEK 3 - BUILD RELATIONSHIPS

Objectives

* Conduct one-on-one meetings
* Build trust
* Understand team concerns
* Identify improvement opportunities

Meet Individually

Engineering Manager

Ask

* Current priorities?
* Delivery challenges?
* Resource constraints?

Developers

Ask

* Biggest blockers?
* Pain points?
* Technical debt?
* Process improvements?

QA Team

Ask

* Testing bottlenecks?
* Quality concerns?
* Automation coverage?

DevOps

Ask

* Release process?
* Deployment issues?
* Infrastructure risks?

Product Owner

Ask

* Business priorities?
* Customer expectations?
* Upcoming roadmap?

Deliverables

* Team Feedback Summary
* Dependency List
* Communication Plan

---

## WEEK 4 - ANALYZE & PLAN

Objectives

* Review findings
* Identify improvement opportunities
* Create action plan

Analyze

* Sprint performance
* Team velocity
* Open risks
* Pending issues
* Technical dependencies
* Communication gaps

Prepare

* Improvement recommendations
* Risk mitigation plan
* Stakeholder communication plan
* Delivery improvement roadmap

Deliverables

* 30-Day Assessment Report
* Risk Register Update
* Action Plan
* Executive Summary

## DO'S

✔ Listen carefully
✔ Build trust
✔ Ask thoughtful questions
✔ Understand before changing
✔ Document observations
✔ Be transparent
✔ Follow up on commitments
✔ Keep stakeholders informed

---

## DON'TS

✘ Don't change processes immediately
✘ Don't micromanage developers
✘ Don't assume existing processes are wrong
✘ Don't ignore team feedback
✘ Don't overpromise timelines
✘ Don't make decisions without understanding the context

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA AFTER 30 DAYS

□ Understand the product and business goals
□ Build strong relationships with stakeholders
□ Understand Agile ceremonies and delivery process
□ Know project risks and dependencies
□ Become familiar with the technology stack
□ Gain visibility into current sprint progress
□ Deliver a 30-Day Assessment Report
□ Create a roadmap for future improvements
