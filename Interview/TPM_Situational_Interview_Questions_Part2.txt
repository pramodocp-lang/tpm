=========================================================
TPM SITUATIONAL INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWERS (PART 2)
Advanced Technical Program Manager Interview Preparation
=========================================================

11. A project dependency is delayed by another team. What would you do?

Answer:
- Identify the impacted deliverables.
- Meet with the dependent team to understand the delay.
- Explore workarounds or parallel activities.
- Update the project plan.
- Communicate the impact to stakeholders.
- Monitor the dependency until it is resolved.

---------------------------------------------------------

12. A critical project risk has become an issue.

Answer:
- Assess the business impact immediately.
- Activate the mitigation or contingency plan.
- Inform stakeholders.
- Assign ownership for resolution.
- Track progress daily.
- Document lessons learned after closure.

---------------------------------------------------------

13. QA finds several critical defects before release.

Answer:
- Prioritize defects by severity.
- Focus on fixing P1 and P2 issues first.
- Re-test all fixes.
- Review release readiness with stakeholders.
- Delay the release if product quality is at risk.

---------------------------------------------------------

14. Team morale is low due to workload.

Answer:
- Meet with team members individually.
- Identify the root cause.
- Rebalance workloads where possible.
- Recognize achievements.
- Encourage work-life balance.
- Maintain regular team communication.

---------------------------------------------------------

15. Multiple projects need the same resources.

Answer:
- Review business priorities.
- Discuss resource allocation with management.
- Optimize project schedules.
- Consider temporary resources if available.
- Keep stakeholders informed of any timeline changes.

---------------------------------------------------------

16. A customer requests a new feature during development.

Answer:
- Understand the business value.
- Estimate effort and impact.
- Review with Product Owner and stakeholders.
- Follow the change management process.
- Add it to the backlog or future sprint based on priority.

---------------------------------------------------------

17. Your project budget is reduced by 20%.

Answer:
- Review project priorities.
- Identify cost-saving opportunities.
- Reduce non-essential scope.
- Optimize resource utilization.
- Discuss trade-offs with stakeholders.
- Update the project plan.

---------------------------------------------------------

18. A vendor fails to deliver on time.

Answer:
- Contact the vendor immediately.
- Understand the reason for the delay.
- Escalate if required.
- Explore alternate vendors if necessary.
- Update stakeholders.
- Revise the delivery schedule.

---------------------------------------------------------

19. The project sponsor asks for a status update unexpectedly.

Answer:
- Present project health clearly.
- Highlight completed work.
- Explain current risks and mitigation plans.
- Share upcoming milestones.
- Mention any support required from leadership.

---------------------------------------------------------

20. Two stakeholders have conflicting priorities.

Answer:
- Meet with both stakeholders.
- Understand their objectives.
- Evaluate business impact.
- Present available options.
- Facilitate a decision based on organizational priorities.

---------------------------------------------------------

21. Your Scrum team is not updating Jira.

Answer:
- Explain the importance of transparency.
- Review Jira usage during Daily Stand-ups.
- Ensure every task has an owner.
- Monitor board hygiene regularly.
- Coach the team on Agile best practices.

---------------------------------------------------------

22. Sprint Velocity has dropped significantly.

Answer:
- Analyze sprint metrics.
- Identify blockers and interruptions.
- Review estimation accuracy.
- Reduce work in progress.
- Improve Sprint Planning and Backlog Refinement.

---------------------------------------------------------

23. A production release fails after deployment.

Answer:
- Activate the incident management process.
- Roll back the release if required.
- Inform stakeholders.
- Coordinate with Engineering and DevOps.
- Conduct Root Cause Analysis (RCA).
- Improve deployment and testing processes.

---------------------------------------------------------

24. A security vulnerability is discovered before release.

Answer:
- Assess the severity immediately.
- Involve the Security team.
- Prioritize remediation.
- Re-test the application.
- Release only after security validation.
- Document preventive measures.

---------------------------------------------------------

25. A senior executive requests additional reports every day.

Answer:
- Understand the reporting expectations.
- Automate dashboards where possible.
- Share concise project health reports.
- Focus on KPIs, risks, milestones, and blockers.
- Maintain a consistent reporting schedule.

---------------------------------------------------------

26. The business team wants faster delivery, but Engineering needs more time.

Answer:
- Facilitate a discussion between both teams.
- Review scope and priorities.
- Consider phased delivery.
- Focus on delivering an MVP first.
- Align expectations through transparent communication.

---------------------------------------------------------

27. The project scope continues to increase without additional budget.

Answer:
- Assess the impact on cost and schedule.
- Prioritize high-value features.
- Discuss trade-offs with stakeholders.
- Seek approval for additional funding or revised timelines.
- Update project documentation.

---------------------------------------------------------

28. Team members are working across multiple time zones.

Answer:
- Establish overlapping working hours.
- Use asynchronous communication tools.
- Document meeting outcomes.
- Rotate meeting schedules fairly.
- Ensure clear ownership of tasks.

---------------------------------------------------------

29. A key stakeholder is not attending project meetings.

Answer:
- Reach out individually.
- Understand the reason.
- Provide concise status updates.
- Escalate if critical decisions are blocked.
- Keep communication proactive.

---------------------------------------------------------

30. You discover a major issue just before Go-Live.

Answer:
- Assess the severity and business impact.
- Discuss options with stakeholders.
- Decide whether to proceed or postpone the release.
- Prioritize customer impact and system stability.
- Communicate the decision clearly.
- Capture lessons learned after resolution.

=========================================================
COMMON TPM INTERVIEW FRAMEWORK

When answering situational questions, follow this structure:

1. Understand the problem.
2. Assess the impact.
3. Prioritize the issue.
4. Communicate with stakeholders.
5. Coordinate cross-functional teams.
6. Execute the resolution plan.
7. Monitor progress.
8. Conduct Root Cause Analysis (RCA).
9. Document lessons learned.
10. Implement preventive actions.
