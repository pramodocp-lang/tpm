# Technical Project Manager (TPM) Interview

## Common Follow-up Questions

========================================

1. EXPERIENCE & CAREER JOURNEY
   ========================================

• Walk me through your career.
• Why did you transition from Software Engineering to Technical Project Management?
• What motivated you to become a TPM?
• What has been your biggest career achievement?
• Tell me about a challenging project you've worked on.
• Which project are you most proud of and why?

========================================
2. CURRENT ROLE
===============

• What are your day-to-day responsibilities?
• How many projects do you manage simultaneously?
• What kind of teams do you work with?
• How large are the teams you manage?
• Who do you report to?
• How do you prioritize your work?

========================================
3. TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE
======================

• Explain the architecture of your recent project.
• How technically involved are you?
• How do you communicate with engineers?
• Have you worked with APIs, microservices, or cloud platforms?
• What is your experience with Agile?
• Which project management and collaboration tools have you used?

========================================
4. PROJECT MANAGEMENT
=====================

• How do you plan a new project?
• How do you estimate project timelines?
• How do you track project progress?
• How do you manage project risks?
• Which KPIs do you monitor?
• How do you handle changing requirements?

========================================
5. STAKEHOLDER MANAGEMENT
=========================

• How do you manage stakeholder expectations?
• Tell me about a difficult stakeholder you worked with.
• How do you communicate project status?
• Have you ever delivered bad news to leadership?
• How do you manage conflicting priorities?

========================================
6. LEADERSHIP
=============

• Describe your leadership style.
• How do you motivate your team?
• How do you resolve conflicts?
• Tell me about a time when a team member was underperforming.
• How do you build trust with cross-functional teams?

========================================
7. DELIVERY & EXECUTION
=======================

• Tell me about a delayed project.
• Describe a project that failed.
• How did you recover from a project setback?
• How do you ensure on-time delivery?
• Have you managed production incidents?

========================================
8. RISK & ISSUE MANAGEMENT
==========================

• Tell me about a major risk you identified early.
• What is the difference between a risk and an issue?
• How do you mitigate project risks?
• When do you escalate an issue?

========================================
9. AGILE & SCRUM
================

• Explain the Agile process followed in your organization.
• Which Scrum ceremonies do you facilitate?
• How do you conduct Sprint Planning?
• How do you handle sprint spillovers?
• Which Agile metrics do you track?

========================================
10. BEHAVIORAL (STAR FORMAT)
============================

• Tell me about a conflict you resolved.
• Tell me about a time you failed.
• Tell me about a difficult decision.
• Tell me about a project under pressure.
• Tell me about a time you influenced without authority.
• Describe a situation where you managed multiple priorities.

========================================
11. METRICS & RESULTS
=====================

• How do you measure project success?
• What is your biggest measurable achievement?
• How much cost or time did you save?
• How do you improve team productivity?

========================================
12. COMPANY & ROLE FIT
======================

• Why do you want to join our company?
• Why are you looking for a change?
• What interests you about this role?
• What value will you bring to our organization?
• Why should we hire you?

========================================
13. CLOSING QUESTIONS
=====================

• What are your strengths?
• What is your biggest weakness?
• Where do you see yourself in five years?
• Do you have any questions for us?

====================================================
TOP 10 MUST-PREPARE QUESTIONS (HIGH PRIORITY)
=============================================

1. Tell me about your current role.
2. Why did you transition from Software Engineer to TPM?
3. Describe one of your recent projects.
4. How do you manage cross-functional teams?
5. How do you manage project risks and dependencies?
6. Explain your Agile delivery process.
7. Tell me about a delayed project and how you recovered it.
8. How do you manage stakeholders with conflicting priorities?
9. What technical knowledge helps you succeed as a TPM?
10. Why do you want to join this company?

Interview Tip:
Prepare every answer using the STAR framework:
• Situation
• Task
• Action
• Result

Aim to keep each answer between 2–3 minutes, highlighting measurable outcomes wherever possible.
