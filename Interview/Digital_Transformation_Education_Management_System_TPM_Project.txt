===============================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
===============================================================

The organization wanted to modernize its education management processes by
replacing multiple manual and paper-based activities with a centralized,
cloud-based Education Management System.

The platform digitized the complete student lifecycle from enquiry to alumni,
improving operational efficiency, collaboration, reporting, and student
experience.

The system was designed to support multiple campuses from a single platform.

===============================================================
TPM INTERVIEW SUMMARY (60 Seconds)
===============================================================

My recent project was a Digital Transformation initiative for an
Education Management System. The objective was to replace manual
academic and administrative processes with a cloud-based SaaS platform.

The platform managed the complete student lifecycle, including enquiry,
counselling, admissions, student registration, fee management,
attendance, examinations, learning progress, notifications, and
reporting.

As the Technical Program Manager, I led a cross-functional team of
25 members and managed project planning, stakeholder communication,
Agile delivery, RAID logs, sprint execution, risk management, release
planning, and production deployments.

One of the biggest challenges was standardizing processes across
multiple campuses while accommodating campus-specific requirements.
By collaborating closely with business and technical teams, we
delivered the project successfully, improving operational efficiency,
reducing admission processing time by around 60%, and enhancing the
overall student experience.
