Executive Communication
-----------------------
Always communicate using this format:

Current Status
Business Impact
Risks
Mitigation Plan
Decision Required
Next Steps

Example:
Project Status : Green

Progress
- Development: 80%
- Testing: 60%

Risk
- Vendor API delay.

Mitigation
- Daily follow-up with vendor.
- Reprioritized sprint tasks.

Decision Required
- Approval for one additional engineering resource.
