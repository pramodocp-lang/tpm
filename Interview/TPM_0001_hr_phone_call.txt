As a Technical Project Manager (TPM), my role is to ensure successful delivery of technical projects 
by coordinating between business and engineering teams.

Define project scope, goals, and timelines
Plan and manage project execution using Agile methodology
Track progress, sprints, and tasks using Jira

Identify and mitigate risks, issues, and dependencies
Ensure on-time delivery with quality output.
