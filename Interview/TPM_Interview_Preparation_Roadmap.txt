===========================================================
TECHNICAL PROGRAM MANAGER (TPM) INTERVIEW PREPARATION ROADMAP
===========================================================

Candidate Profile
-----------------
Experience:
- 14+ years in Software Engineering
- 6+ years in Technical Project/Program Management

Objective:
Prepare for Technical Program Manager (TPM) interviews in Product, SaaS,
Cloud, Data Engineering, and AI-driven organizations.

===========================================================
ROUND 1: INTRODUCTION & EXPERIENCE
===========================================================

Objective:
Assess communication skills, confidence, and career journey.

Prepare for:
- Tell me about yourself.
- Walk me through your resume.
- Why are you looking for a change?
- Why do you want to join our company?
- What is your biggest achievement?
- Describe a challenging project.
- Why should we hire you?

Preparation Goal:
- Deliver a confident 2–3 minute introduction.
- Highlight leadership, technical expertise, and business impact.

===========================================================
ROUND 2: TECHNICAL PROGRAM MANAGEMENT
===========================================================

Topics:
- Program Management Lifecycle
- Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
- Agile & Scrum
- Kanban
- Waterfall vs Agile
- Sprint Planning
- Release Planning
- PI Planning
- RAID Management
- Dependency Management
- Stakeholder Management
- Governance
- Engineering Metrics
- Risk Management
- Escalation Management

Sample Questions:
- Describe a complex program you managed.
- How do you handle competing priorities?
- How do you manage cross-functional teams?
- How do you track project health?
- How do you identify and mitigate risks?
- How do you handle delivery delays?

ROUND 4: BEHAVIORAL INTERVIEW
===========================================================

Preparation Method:
STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result)

Practice Questions:
- Tell me about a conflict.
- Describe a failed project.
- Tell me about a difficult stakeholder.
- Describe a leadership challenge.
- Tell me about an escalation.
- Describe an unhappy customer.
- Explain a missed project deadline.

ROUND 10: SCENARIO-BASED QUESTIONS
