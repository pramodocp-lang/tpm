TPM SITUATIONAL INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWERS (PART 3)
Senior Technical Program Manager Interview Preparation
Cloud | AI/ML | Digital Transformation | Infrastructure
=========================================================

31. A Cloud Migration project is delayed because the infrastructure team has not completed server provisioning.

Answer:
- Meet with the Infrastructure team to understand the delay.
- Identify the impact on dependent activities.
- Explore temporary environments if possible.
- Update the project schedule.
- Communicate the revised timeline to stakeholders.
- Monitor progress until infrastructure is ready.

---------------------------------------------------------

32. Business wants to Go-Live, but QA has not signed off.

Answer:
- Review all open defects.
- Assess business and technical risks.
- Discuss options with QA and Business.
- Recommend postponing the release if critical issues remain.
- Obtain formal approval before any Go-Live decision.

---------------------------------------------------------

33. A critical API integration fails during User Acceptance Testing (UAT).

Answer:
- Investigate logs and error reports.
- Coordinate with both application teams.
- Prioritize the issue.
- Track resolution through daily checkpoints.
- Re-test after fixes.
- Update stakeholders on progress.

---------------------------------------------------------

34. A production deployment fails because of configuration issues.

Answer:
- Stop further deployment.
- Roll back to the previous stable version.
- Coordinate with DevOps.
- Correct configuration errors.
- Validate deployment in staging.
- Schedule a controlled deployment.

Answer:

- Review Jira dashboards and RAID Logs.
- Identify risks and quick wins.
- Improve communication.
- Build trust across teams.
- Deliver measurable improvements.

=========================================================
TPM DECISION-MAKING FRAMEWORK

Whenever you answer a situational question, remember this sequence:

1. Understand the situation.
2. Gather facts and data.
3. Assess business impact.
4. Identify risks and dependencies.
5. Communicate with stakeholders.
6. Evaluate available options.
7. Make a data-driven decision.
8. Execute the plan.
9. Monitor progress.
10. Capture lessons learned.

=========================================================
FINAL INTERVIEW TIP

For every answer:
- Stay calm and structured.
- Show ownership.
- Emphasize communication.
- Focus on collaboration.
- Explain your decision-making process.
- Highlight measurable outcomes whenever possible.

A strong TPM answer demonstrates:
- Leadership
- Problem-solving
- Stakeholder management
- Risk management
- Technical understanding
- Business alignment
- Delivery focus
