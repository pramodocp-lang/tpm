TECHNICAL PROJECT MANAGER (TPM) – DAILY ACTIVITIES

====================================================
09:00 AM – 09:30 AM | Project Health Review
====================================================

• Review project dashboards and KPIs
• Check Jira boards and sprint status
• Review emails, escalations, and urgent requests
• Identify blockers, risks, and critical issues
• Validate project health indicators

====================================================
09:30 AM – 10:00 AM | Daily Scrum / Stand-up
====================================================

• Facilitate daily stand-up meeting
• Review progress against sprint goals
• Discuss blockers and dependencies
• Capture action items and ownership
• Ensure team alignment on priorities

====================================================
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM | Stakeholder Management
====================================================

• Communicate project status updates
• Coordinate with Product, Engineering, QA, and Business teams
• Review delivery timelines and expectations
• Resolve cross-functional dependencies
• Manage stakeholder concerns

====================================================
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM | Sprint Execution & Delivery Tracking
====================================================

• Review active user stories and tasks
• Monitor sprint burndown and velocity
• Track milestone progress
• Validate delivery commitments
• Follow up on pending development activities

====================================================
12:00 PM – 01:00 PM | Risk & Issue Management
====================================================

• Maintain RAID Log
• Review project risks and mitigation plans
• Track issues and dependencies
• Escalate critical concerns when necessary
• Monitor project governance activities

====================================================
01:00 PM – 02:00 PM | Lunch Break
====================================================

====================================================
02:00 PM – 03:00 PM | Project Planning & Resource Management
====================================================

• Capacity planning
• Resource allocation review
• Dependency management
• Release planning
• Sprint forecasting and roadmap alignment

====================================================
03:00 PM – 04:00 PM | Technical Coordination
====================================================

• Architecture and design discussions
• API and integration reviews
• Environment readiness verification
• Deployment planning
• Technical risk assessment

====================================================
04:00 PM – 05:00 PM | Client & Business Meetings
====================================================

• Requirement clarification sessions
• Change Request (CR) discussions
• Delivery commitment reviews
• Business alignment meetings
• Project status presentations

====================================================
05:00 PM – 05:30 PM | Documentation & Reporting
====================================================

• Update Confluence documentation
• Prepare Meeting Minutes (MoM)
• Track action items
• Update project reports
• Maintain project artifacts

====================================================
05:30 PM – 06:00 PM | End-of-Day Review
====================================================

• Review completed activities
• Follow up on open actions
• Prepare next-day priorities
• Update project dashboard
• Verify project health status
