ROLE: Technical Project Manager (TPM)

1. Core Responsibility
- Own end-to-end delivery of technical projects from initiation to closure

2. Key Focus Areas

A. Planning
- Define scope, milestones, timelines
- Break work into epics and dependencies

B. Execution Management
- Drive engineering, QA, product alignment
- Track sprint execution and delivery progress

C. Stakeholder Management
- Align business, product, engineering, QA teams
- Communicate status, risks, and decisions

D. Risk & Issue Management
- Identify risks early
- Maintain RAID log
- Drive mitigation actions

E. Delivery Governance
- Run standups, sprint reviews, retrospectives
- Ensure quality, timeline, and scope adherence

F. Cross-functional Coordination
- Remove blockers and manage dependencies across teams

G. Reporting & Metrics
- Track KPIs (velocity, burn-down, delivery health)
- Provide dashboards and executive updates

3. End Goal
- Deliver projects on time, within scope, with quality and business impact
