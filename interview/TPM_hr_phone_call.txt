HR PHONE SCREENING – SHORT & NATURAL ANSWERS

1. Tell me about yourself

I am a Technical Project Manager with experience in managing end-to-end project delivery.  
I collaborate closely with engineering, QA, product, and business teams to ensure timely and high-quality delivery of projects.  
My core focus areas include project planning, stakeholder management, risk mitigation, and successful execution of initiatives.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Why are you looking for a change?

I am looking for new challenges, opportunities to work on larger projects, and a role where I can continue to grow professionally.

--------------------------------------------------

3. Why do you want to join our company?

I am impressed by the company's products and growth. The role aligns well with my experience, and I believe it offers a good opportunity to contribute and learn.

--------------------------------------------------

4. Tell me about a challenging project.

We had a critical release with tight timelines and dependencies across teams. Through regular tracking and communication, we managed the risks and delivered the release successfully.

--------------------------------------------------

5. How do you handle conflicts?

I focus on understanding different perspectives, encouraging open communication, and finding a solution that supports the project goals.

--------------------------------------------------

6. How do you handle pressure?

I prioritize tasks, stay organized, and communicate proactively. Clear planning helps me manage pressure effectively.

--------------------------------------------------

7. What are your strengths?

• Stakeholder Management
• Communication
• Project Planning
• Problem Solving
• Risk Management

--------------------------------------------------

8. What is your weakness?

Sometimes I spend extra time on planning, but I've learned to balance planning with execution.

--------------------------------------------------

9. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

I see myself taking on larger programs and leadership responsibilities while continuing to grow as a project management professional.

--------------------------------------------------

10. Why should we hire you?

I bring project delivery experience, strong coordination skills, and the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams to achieve business goals.
