May I ask a few questions about the role and the organization?

This is a great question to ask at the end of an HR interview. Some good follow-up questions are:

Could you please tell me more about the key responsibilities of this role?
What are the current projects or initiatives that this position will be working on?
How is success measured for this role in the first 3 to 6 months?
Can you tell me about the team structure and reporting hierarchy?
What are the next steps in the interview process?
