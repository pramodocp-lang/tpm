1. DOCUMENT PURPOSE
========================================================================

This document provides the High-Level Design (HLD) of the Enterprise
Educational CRM System. It describes the overall architecture, business
modules, user roles, integrations, workflows, and technology stack.

The system supports the complete student lifecycle from enquiry to
admission, visa processing, enrollment, alumni management, and reporting.
