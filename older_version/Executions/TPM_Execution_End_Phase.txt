=========================================================
TPM EXECUTION END PHASE (PROJECT COMPLETION STAGE)
=========================================================

1. WHAT IS EXECUTION END PHASE?

This is the final stage of the execution lifecycle where
the project is formally completed and closed after delivery.

Goal:
- Confirm successful delivery
- Close all activities
- Validate outcomes
- Transition to support/maintenance

=========================================================

2. TPM ROLE IN EXECUTION END

TPM ensures structured closure of the project.

Key responsibilities:

1. Confirm final delivery completion
2. Ensure all features are delivered
3. Close open tasks in Jira
4. Verify defect closure
5. Get formal sign-off
6. Conduct retrospective
7. Document lessons learned
8. Close project reporting

=========================================================

3. FINAL DELIVERY CONFIRMATION

TPM checks:

- All committed scope delivered
- Sprint goals achieved
- No critical pending work

Output:
- Delivery completion confirmation

=========================================================

4. DEFECT CLOSURE

TPM ensures:

- All critical and high defects resolved
- Remaining minor defects documented
- No blocking issues remain

=========================================================

5. FORMAL SIGN-OFF

TPM collects approval from:

- Product Owner
- Business Unit Head
- Stakeholders

Sign-off confirms:
- Project is successfully delivered
- Business goals are met

=========================================================

6. RETROSPECTIVE MEETING

TPM conducts retrospective with teams:

Discuss:
- What went well
- What went wrong
- What can be improved

Goal:
- Improve future delivery process

=========================================================

7. DOCUMENTATION COMPLETION

TPM ensures:

- Final reports are created
- Confluence documentation updated
- Project artifacts stored
- Knowledge transfer completed

=========================================================

8. SUPPORT TRANSITION

TPM coordinates handover to support team:

Includes:
- System overview
- Known issues
- Deployment details
- Support contacts

=========================================================

9. FINAL REPORTING

TPM prepares final project report:

Includes:
- Project summary
- Timeline performance
- Budget usage
- Risk summary
- Defect summary
- Delivery status

=========================================================

10. PROJECT CLOSURE METRICS

- Delivery success rate
- Defect leakage
- Timeline adherence
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Sprint performance

=========================================================

11. TPM INTERVIEW ANSWER

Q: What do you do at the end of execution?

Answer:

At the end of execution, I ensure all deliverables are
completed, defects are closed, and formal sign-off is
obtained from stakeholders. I conduct a retrospective,
document lessons learned, complete project reporting,
and ensure smooth transition to support teams.

=========================================================

12. ONE-LINE SUMMARY

Execution end phase ensures final delivery confirmation,
defect closure, stakeholder sign-off, and smooth project
closure with lessons learned.
