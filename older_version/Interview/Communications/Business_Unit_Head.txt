=========================================================
BUSINESS UNIT HEAD - TPM GUIDE
=========================================================

1. WHO IS A BUSINESS UNIT HEAD?

A Business Unit Head (BU Head) is a senior leader who
manages a specific business division within an organization.

They are responsible for the performance, growth, and
strategy of that business unit.

=========================================================

2. SIMPLE MEANING

A Business Unit Head is the person who:

- Owns a business division or product line
- Drives revenue and growth
- Makes strategic decisions
- Leads senior managers and teams
- Ensures business goals are achieved

=========================================================

3. EXAMPLE OF BUSINESS UNITS

In a technology company:

Business Unit A → Cloud Services
Business Unit B → Mobile Applications
Business Unit C → AI Products

Each unit has its own Business Unit Head.

=========================================================

4. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

A. Business Strategy
- Define vision and goals for the business unit
- Align with company strategy

B. Revenue & Growth
- Increase revenue
- Identify new opportunities
- Improve profitability

C. Team Leadership
- Manage managers and senior leaders
- Oversee team performance

D. Budget Management
- Control budget allocation
- Optimize costs

E. Stakeholder Communication
- Report to executives (CEO, CTO, etc.)
- Coordinate with cross-functional teams

=========================================================

5. TPM INTERACTION WITH BU HEAD

A TPM works with the Business Unit Head to:

- Provide high-level project updates
- Highlight risks impacting business goals
- Discuss delivery timelines
- Align technical execution with business priorities
- Support decision-making

=========================================================

6. WHY BU HEAD IS IMPORTANT

- Drives business direction
- Owns financial outcomes
- Ensures strategic alignment
- Influences major decisions
- Oversees multiple teams and projects

=========================================================

7. SIMPLE EXAMPLE

If a TPM is managing a CRM project:

The BU Head will care about:
- Is the project increasing customer acquisition?
- Is delivery on time?
- What is the business impact?
- Are we within budget?

=========================================================

8. TPM INTERVIEW ANSWER

Q: Who is a Business Unit Head?

Answer:

A Business Unit Head is a senior leader responsible for
managing a specific business division, driving its growth,
strategy, revenue, and overall performance. They ensure
alignment with organizational goals and oversee teams,
budgets, and delivery outcomes within their unit.

=========================================================

9. ONE-LINE SUMMARY

A Business Unit Head owns and drives the performance,
strategy, and growth of a specific business division.
