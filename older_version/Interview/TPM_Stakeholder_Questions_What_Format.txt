=========================================================
TPM STAKEHOLDER QUESTIONS (STARTING WITH "WHAT")
=========================================================

EXECUTIVE SPONSORS
-------------------------
What strategic objectives does this project support?
What business value is expected from this initiative?
What KPIs should be reported to leadership?
What decisions require executive approval?
What risks should be escalated immediately?

BUSINESS OWNERS
-------------------------
What business problems are we solving?
What outcomes define project success?
What processes will be impacted?
What business priorities should guide decisions?
What constraints should the team be aware of?

CLIENTS / CUSTOMERS
-------------------------
What are your expectations from the project?
What deliverables are most important to you?
What concerns do you have regarding scope or timeline?
What success criteria should be met?
What additional value would you like to see?

PRODUCT MANAGERS / PRODUCT OWNERS
-------------------------
What user problems are being addressed?
What features are highest priority?
What requirements are mandatory for release?
What assumptions need validation?
What product metrics will measure success?

PROJECT MANAGER / TPM
-------------------------
What milestones are approaching?
What dependencies require attention?
What blockers need escalation?
What risks require mitigation?
What actions are needed to maintain project health?

ENGINEERING MANAGERS
-------------------------
What resources are available for the project?
What technical challenges may impact delivery?
What skills gaps need attention?
What support does the team require?
What dependencies exist across teams?

SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS
-------------------------

What technical risks have been identified?

What support is needed to complete tasks?
What improvements can increase productivity?

-------------------------

What integrations are required?
What scalability considerations exist?
What security requirements must be implemented?
What technical constraints affect the solution?

What defects are critical for release?
What test coverage goals should be achieved?

What infrastructure changes are needed?
