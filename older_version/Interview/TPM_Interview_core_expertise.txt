TPM CORE EXPERTISE → INTERVIEW QUESTIONS MAPPING

------------------------------------------------------------

1. Project Planning, Execution & Delivery
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you plan a project from scratch?
- How do you define milestones and track delivery?
- Tell me about a project you delivered end-to-end.
- What do you do when delivery is slipping?

Focus Area:
Delivery ownership, planning, execution, milestone tracking


------------------------------------------------------------

2. SDLC Management & Delivery Governance
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- Explain how SDLC works in your project.
- How do you ensure quality at each SDLC phase?
- What governance mechanisms do you use?
- How do you track project health?

Focus Area:
Process control, quality gates, governance, monitoring


------------------------------------------------------------

3. Waterfall / Agile / Scrum / Hybrid
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- When do you choose Agile vs Waterfall?
- What is your experience with Scrum ceremonies?
- Have you worked in hybrid models? Why?
- What challenges have you faced in Agile?

Focus Area:
Delivery methodology understanding & adaptability


------------------------------------------------------------

4. Risk & Dependency Management
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- Tell me about a major risk you handled.
- How do you identify risks early?
- How do you manage cross-team dependencies?
- What tools do you use for tracking risks?

Focus Area:
Risk identification, mitigation, dependency control


------------------------------------------------------------

5. Sprint & Release Planning
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you plan a sprint?
- What is your role in sprint ceremonies?
- How do you handle scope changes mid-sprint?
- How do you coordinate releases?

Focus Area:
Agile execution, sprint planning, release coordination


------------------------------------------------------------

6. Resource & Capacity Planning
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you allocate resources?
- What happens when teams are overloaded?
- How do you forecast capacity?
- How do you handle resource conflicts?

Focus Area:
Resource optimization, capacity forecasting, team allocation


------------------------------------------------------------

7. Stakeholder Management
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you handle difficult stakeholders?
- How do you communicate delays?
- How do you manage expectations?
- Example of stakeholder conflict?

Focus Area:
Communication, alignment, expectation management


------------------------------------------------------------

8. Cross-Functional Leadership
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you align multiple teams?
- Example of cross-team coordination?
- How do you handle conflicting priorities?
- How do you ensure alignment without authority?

Focus Area:
Coordination, influence, leadership without authority


------------------------------------------------------------

9. Team Collaboration
------------------------------------------------------------
Questions:
- How do you ensure team collaboration?
- How do you handle communication gaps?
- What if team members are not aligned?
- How do you improve productivity?

Focus Area:
Team dynamics, communication, execution efficiency


------------------------------------------------------------

TPM INTERVIEW SUMMARY
------------------------------------------------------------
Everything maps into 4 pillars:

1. Delivery Ownership (Planning, Execution, Sprint, Release)
2. Process Control (SDLC, Governance, Agile/Waterfall)
3. Risk Control (Risks, Dependencies, Capacity)
4. People Leadership (Stakeholders, Teams, Collaboration)
