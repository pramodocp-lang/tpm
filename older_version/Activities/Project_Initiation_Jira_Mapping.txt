EPIC: INITIATION PHASE – PROJECT DEFINITION & APPROVAL

Goal:
Define and approve project before planning phase.


STORY 1: Business Problem Identification
- Capture problem statement
- Validate with sponsor
Acceptance:
- Approved problem statement


STORY 2: Business Case Creation
- ROI definition
- Cost estimation
- Feasibility check
Acceptance:
- Business case approved


STORY 3: High-Level Scope Definition
- In scope / Out of scope
Acceptance:
- Scope approved


STORY 4: Stakeholder Mapping
- Identify stakeholders
- Create register
Acceptance:
- Stakeholder list finalized


STORY 5: Project Charter Creation
- Compile initiation inputs
- Get approvals
Acceptance:
- Signed charter


STORY 6: Risk & Assumption Logging
- Identify risks
- Document assumptions
Acceptance:
- RAID log updated


STORY 7: Initiation Closure
- Verify exit criteria
- Get final sign-off
Acceptance:
- Phase completed
