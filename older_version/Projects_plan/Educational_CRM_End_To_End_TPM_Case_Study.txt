=========================================================
1. PROJECT BACKGROUND
=========================================================

The organization was managing student admissions,
follow-ups, attendance, mentor assignments, and fee
collections manually using Excel sheets and emails.

Business users faced several challenges:

- Duplicate lead records
- Missed student follow-ups
- Manual admission tracking
- No centralized reporting
- Lack of visibility into branch performance
- Revenue leakage due to fee tracking issues

Leadership wanted a centralized CRM platform to
streamline the entire student lifecycle.

=========================================================
2. BUSINESS OBJECTIVE
=========================================================

Build a centralized Educational CRM system that:

- Manages student leads
- Tracks admissions
- Handles mentor assignments
- Monitors attendance
- Manages fee collections
- Sends notifications
- Provides dashboards and reports

Expected Outcomes:

- Increase lead conversion by 25%
- Reduce manual effort by 50%
- Improve reporting visibility
- Improve student engagement

18. TPM INTERVIEW ANSWER
(TELL ME ABOUT A PROJECT YOU MANAGED)
=========================================================

I managed an Educational CRM implementation aimed
at digitizing the student admission lifecycle.

The project involved lead management, student
enrollment, mentor assignments, attendance,
payments, communication, and reporting.

As the TPM, I led requirement gathering,
planning, sprint execution, stakeholder
management, risk mitigation, release planning,
and production deployment.

The project was delivered using Agile Scrum
across six sprints with a cross-functional team
of 12 members.

The solution improved lead conversion by 28%,
reduced manual effort by 55%, and increased
reporting visibility across all branches.

The project was delivered on time and within
budget while achieving over 90% user adoption.
