EDUCATIONAL CRM SYSTEM - PROJECT PLANNING (PHASE 1)
====================================================

1. PROJECT NAME
----------------
Educational CRM System Enhancement Project

----------------------------------------------------

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
---------------------
Current educational CRM system lacks centralized tracking of students, counselors, and branch-level reporting.
This leads to:
- Manual reporting effort
- Data inconsistency across branches
- Delayed decision-making for management

----------------------------------------------------

3. PROJECT GOAL (SMART OBJECTIVES)
-----------------------------------
- Improve student lifecycle tracking by 40%
- Reduce manual reporting effort by 50%
- Enable real-time dashboards for Head Office (HO)
- Improve counselor productivity and lead conversion visibility

----------------------------------------------------

4. STAKEHOLDERS
---------------
- Head Office (HO)
- Branch Office Managers
- Counsellors
- Front Office Team
- Mentors
- Product Team
- Engineering Team
- QA Team
- Support / Operations Team

----------------------------------------------------

5. SCOPE DEFINITION

IN SCOPE:
---------
- Student onboarding and lead management
- Role-based dashboards (Counsellor, Branch, HO)
- Reporting system
- Workflow automation
- Notifications and reminders

OUT OF SCOPE:
-------------
- Payment gateway integration (future phase)
- Mobile application development (Phase 2)
- AI-based recommendation system (future enhancement)

----------------------------------------------------

- Prepare high-level system architecture
- Plan sprint timeline
