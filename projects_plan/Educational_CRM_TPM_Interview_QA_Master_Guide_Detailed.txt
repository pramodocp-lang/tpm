Q4. HOW DO YOU HANDLE CHANGING REQUIREMENTS?
=========================================================

I follow a structured change management process.

Steps:

1. Capture Change Request
2. Analyze Impact
3. Estimate Effort
4. Review with Stakeholders
5. Obtain Approval
6. Schedule in Sprint

Example:

A request was raised to add branch-wise revenue
dashboards after Sprint 3.

I assessed the impact, discussed trade-offs,
and included it in Sprint 5 without affecting
the release timeline.
