# LINKEDIN & PROFESSIONAL ANNOUNCEMENT PHRASES

1. I am happy to share that...

---

Meaning:
Expresses happiness while sharing good news.

Tone:
Friendly, positive, professional.

Best Used:

* New job
* Certification
* Promotion
* Project completion
* Personal milestone

Example:
I am happy to share that I have joined ABC Technologies as a Technical Project Manager.


==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

2. I am pleased to share that...

---

Meaning:
A more formal and polished version of "happy."

Tone:
Professional, respectful, corporate.

Best Used:

* Official announcements
* Company updates
* Client communication
* Professional achievements

Example:
I am pleased to share that I have successfully completed my PMP certification.


==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

3. I am excited to share that...

---

Meaning:
Shows enthusiasm and energy.

Tone:
Enthusiastic, confident.

Best Used:

* New role
* Career change
* Product launch
* Speaking engagements

Example:
I am excited to share that I have joined XYZ Solutions as a Technical Project Manager.


==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

4. I am thrilled to share that...

---

Meaning:
Expresses great excitement.

Tone:
Highly enthusiastic.

Best Used:

* Dream job
* Major achievement
* Big career milestone

Example:

==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

5. I am delighted to share that...

---

Meaning:
Shows genuine joy and satisfaction.

Tone:
Warm, elegant, appreciative.

Best Used:

* Awards
* Promotions

* Career milestones

Example:

==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

6. I am proud to share that...

---

Meaning:
Highlights personal accomplishment and hard work.

Tone:
Confident but professional.

Best Used:

7. I am honored to share that...

---

Meaning:
Shows humility and gratitude for recognition.

Tone:
Respectful and appreciative.

Best Used:

==========================================================================================================================

8. I am grateful to share that...

---

Meaning:
Expresses appreciation for opportunities received.

Tone:
Humble, thankful.

Best Used:

Example:

==========================================================================================================================

9. Excited to announce that...

---

Meaning:
A modern LinkedIn opening without using "I am."

Tone:
Energetic and engaging.

Best Used:

* Job updates
* Product launches
* Company news
* Career announcements

Example:
Excited to announce that I have joined ABC Technologies as a Technical Project Manager.

==========================================================================================================================

10. Delighted to announce that...

---

Meaning:
A polished announcement expressing happiness.

Tone:
Professional and positive.

Best Used:

* Company announcements
* Career updates
* Milestones

Example:

==========================================================================================================================

11. Proud to announce that...

---

Meaning:
Focuses on achievement and accomplishment.

Tone:
Confident and celebratory.

Best Used:

* Certifications

* Major project completion

Example:

12. It gives me great pleasure to announce that...

---

Meaning:
Very formal and ceremonial.

Tone:
Official and prestigious.

Best Used:

* Leadership announcements
* Formal events

Example:

==========================================================================================================================

13. I would like to share that...

---

Meaning:
A neutral and straightforward introduction.

Tone:
Simple, formal, professional.

Best Used:

* Professional communication
* Emails

Example:

==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

14. I’m excited to begin a new chapter...

---

Meaning:
Emphasizes growth and a fresh start.

Tone:
Inspirational and optimistic.

Best Used:

* New job
* Career transition
* Relocation
* Entrepreneurship

Example:

==========================================================================================================================
==========================================================================================================================

15. With gratitude, I would like to share...

---

Meaning:
Combines appreciation with a positive announcement.

Tone:
Humble, sincere, professional.

Best Used:

* Thanking mentors
* Career opportunities
* Personal achievements

Example:
