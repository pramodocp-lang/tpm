===============================================================================
TECHNICAL PROJECT MANAGER (TPM)
END-TO-END SOFTWARE DELIVERY LIFECYCLE
======================================

## OBJECTIVE

Understand where each stakeholder enters the project, what they own,
their key deliverables, and where the TPM is responsible.

===============================================================================
PHASE 1 - BUSINESS IDEA / INITIATION
====================================

## Primary Owner

• Business Sponsor
• Business Leadership

## Stakeholders Enter

• Business Sponsor
• Executive Leadership

## Responsibilities

Business Sponsor
• Identify business problem
• Define business objectives
• Secure budget
• Approve project initiation

## Deliverables

• Business Vision
• Business Case
• Initial Budget
• Executive Approval

## TPM Role

Usually not involved or only informed at this stage.

===============================================================================
PHASE 2 - REQUIREMENT GATHERING
===============================

## Primary Owner

• Business Analyst (BA)
OR
• Product Manager (PM)

## Stakeholders Enter

• Business Sponsor
• Business Users
• Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)
• Business Analyst
• Product Manager
• Technical Project Manager
• Solution Architect (if required)

## Responsibilities

Business Sponsor
• Explain business objectives
• Define expected outcomes

Business Users
• Explain current process
• Describe pain points
• Share business expectations

Business Analyst
• Conduct workshops
• Gather business requirements
• Document BRD

Product Manager
• Understand market needs
• Define product vision
• Prioritize business needs

Technical Project Manager
• Participate in requirement workshops
• Understand business goals
• Ask technical questions
• Identify risks
• Identify dependencies
• Capture assumptions
• Ensure requirements are clear

Solution Architect
• Provide technical guidance
• Highlight architectural constraints

## Deliverables

• BRD
• Meeting Notes
• Initial Scope
• Stakeholder Register

TPM RESPONSIBILITY STARTS HERE
==============================

✓ Participate in requirement gathering
✓ Review and analyze requirements
✓ Validate technical feasibility
✓ Identify dependencies
✓ Assess risks
✓ Coordinate engineering estimates
✓ Create project plan
✓ Plan releases and milestones
✓ Lead execution
✓ Track delivery
✓ Remove blockers
✓ Coordinate testing
✓ Manage release
✓ Communicate with stakeholders
✓ Close the project successfully

===============================================================================
ONE-LINE SUMMARY
================

Business Sponsor  -> Defines WHY the project is needed.
Business Analyst  -> Documents business requirements (BRD).
Product Manager   -> Defines WHAT should be built (PRD).
Solution Architect-> Designs HOW the solution will work.
Engineering Team  -> Builds the solution.
QA Team           -> Verifies the solution.
DevOps            -> Deploys the solution.
Technical Project Manager -> Plans, coordinates, tracks, manages risks,
dependencies, communication, and ensures successful end-to-end delivery.
