=========================================================
TPM PROJECT CLOSURE PHASE
=========================================================

1. WHAT IS PROJECT CLOSURE PHASE?

Project Closure is the final stage of the project lifecycle
where all activities are formally completed, documented,
and handed over.

Goal:
- Formally close the project
- Ensure all deliverables are completed
- Transfer knowledge to support teams
- Capture lessons learned

=========================================================

2. TPM ROLE IN CLOSURE PHASE

TPM ensures structured and complete closure of the project.

Key responsibilities:

1. Confirm all deliverables are completed
2. Ensure no open critical tasks remain
3. Close all Jira tickets
4. Validate defect closure
5. Collect stakeholder sign-off
6. Conduct retrospective meeting
7. Complete final documentation
8. Ensure handover to support/operations team

=========================================================

3. DELIVERABLE COMPLETION CHECK

TPM verifies:

- All features delivered
- All user stories completed
- No pending sprint work
- Acceptance criteria met

Output:
- Delivery completion confirmation

=========================================================

4. DEFECT CLOSURE

TPM ensures:

- Critical defects = 0
- High priority defects = 0
- Remaining low defects documented or deferred

=========================================================

5. STAKEHOLDER SIGN-OFF

TPM collects formal approval from:

- Product Owner
- Business Unit Head
- Business Stakeholders

Sign-off confirms:
- Project is accepted
- Business goals achieved
- Delivery is complete

=========================================================

6. RETROSPECTIVE MEETING

TPM conducts project retrospective:

Discuss:
- What went well
- What did not go well
- What can be improved

Goal:
- Improve future project delivery

=========================================================

7. DOCUMENTATION COMPLETION

TPM ensures:

- Final project report is prepared
- Confluence pages are updated
- Technical documents are stored
- Knowledge base is created

=========================================================

8. HANDOVER TO SUPPORT TEAM

TPM coordinates transition to operations/support team:

Includes:
- System overview
- Known issues
- Deployment details
- Monitoring process
- Support contacts

=========================================================

9. FINAL PROJECT REPORT

Includes:

- Project summary
- Timeline performance
- Budget usage
- Risk summary
- Issue summary
- Delivery success status

=========================================================

10. PROJECT CLOSURE METRICS

- Delivery success rate
- Defect leakage rate
- Timeline adherence
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Sprint completion rate

=========================================================

11. TPM INTERVIEW ANSWER

Q: What do you do in project closure?

Answer:

In project closure, I ensure all deliverables are completed,
defects are closed, and formal sign-off is obtained from
stakeholders. I conduct a retrospective meeting, document
lessons learned, complete final reporting, and ensure smooth
handover to the support team.

=========================================================

12. ONE-LINE SUMMARY

Project closure is the final phase where deliverables are
completed, sign-off is obtained, documentation is finished,
and the project is formally closed.
