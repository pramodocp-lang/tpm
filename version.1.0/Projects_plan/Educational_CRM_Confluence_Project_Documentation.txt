=========================================================
1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
=========================================================

Project Name:
Educational CRM System

Project Type:
Web-Based CRM Application

Business Goal:
Provide a centralized platform to manage
student leads, admissions, mentor assignments,
attendance, fee collection, communication,
and reporting.

Expected Benefits:
- Improve lead conversion rate
- Reduce manual effort
- Increase operational visibility
- Improve student engagement
- Centralize business data

=========================================================
2. BUSINESS PROBLEM STATEMENT
=========================================================

Current Challenges:

- Lead data managed in Excel files
- Student records scattered across systems
- Manual attendance tracking
- Fee collection not centralized
- Limited reporting visibility
- Communication handled manually

Business Impact:

- Delayed admissions
- Data inconsistencies
- Missed follow-ups
- Revenue leakage
- Poor management reporting

=========================================================
3. PROJECT OBJECTIVES
=========================================================

Objective 1:
Digitize complete admission lifecycle.

Objective 2:

Objective 3:

Objective 4:
