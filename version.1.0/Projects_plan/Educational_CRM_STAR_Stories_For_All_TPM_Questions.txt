STAR STORY 1
TELL ME ABOUT A PROJECT YOU MANAGED
=========================================================

SITUATION

The organization was managing student admissions,
lead tracking, attendance, and fee collection
through Excel sheets and emails.

This resulted in duplicate records, missed
follow-ups, reporting issues, and operational
inefficiencies.

---------------------------------------------------------

TASK

As TPM, I was responsible for delivering a
centralized Educational CRM platform within
six months.

---------------------------------------------------------

ACTION

- Conducted stakeholder workshops
- Defined project scope
- Created roadmap and release plan
- Established Jira workflows
- Managed sprint execution
- Tracked risks and dependencies
- Coordinated cross-functional teams
- Led UAT and production deployment

---------------------------------------------------------

RESULT

- Lead conversion increased by 28%
- Manual effort reduced by 55%
- User adoption exceeded 90%
- Project delivered on schedule
